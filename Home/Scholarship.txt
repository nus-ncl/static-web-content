                        <p align="Center"><b><font size="6px" color="#0d6dba">Launch of CSA-National Cybersecurity R&amp;D Lab Scholarship (NCLS)</font>                               </b></p>
                    </div>
            

                <p>&nbsp;</p>
                <p><strong>OVERVIEW</strong></p>
                <p>The CSA-National Cybersecurity R&amp;D Lab Scholarship (NCLS) aims to drive and support the development of professionals in Singapore’s cybersecurity sector and support the National Cybersecurity R&amp;D Lab (NCL) initiative. This scholarship (funded by the <a href="https://www.csa.gov.sg/">Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA)</a>) will be awarded to qualified students who are enrolled in NUS’s Master’s programmes focusing on cybersecurity.</p>
                <p>&nbsp;</p>

                <p><strong>ELIGIBILITY</strong></p>
                <p>This scholarship is open to students who:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Are Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents.</li>
                    <li>Are admitted into NUS Masters programme with emphasis on cybersecurity (full-time or part-time).</li>
                </ul>
                <p>&nbsp;</p>

                <p><strong>BENEFITS OF NCLS PROGRAMME</strong></p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Tuition fees (after MOE Tuition Fee Grant) of NUS Master of Computing or related course programme.</li>
                    <li>Full-time students are eligible for monthly stipends capped at 18 months.</li>
                    <li>The monthly stipend amount is $3500 for Singapore Citizens and $2700 for Singapore Permanent Residents</li>
                    <li>A project grant of $5,000 to the academic supervisors of the students’ cybersecurity projects or dissertations.</li>
                </ul>
                <p>&nbsp;</p>
                
                <p><strong>CONDITIONS OF NCLS</strong></p>
                <p>Students who are awarded the scholarship must:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Maintain their Cumulative Average Point (CAP) at average 4.0 and have good academic standing.</li>
                    <li>Post-graduation, be employed at NCL for a period of at least 1 year, or embark on their PhD studies in a Singapore University, or be employed in Singapore’s cybersecurity industry for at least 1 year.</li>
                </ul>
                <p>&nbsp;</p>
                
                <p><strong>ELIGIBLE NUS MASTER’S PROGRAMMES</strong></p>
                <ul>
                    <li><a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/#mcomp_spln" target="_blank"><u>Master of Computing</u></a> (General Track) and working on either a dissertation, or a 2-semester project related to cybersecurity.</li>
                    <li><a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/#mcomp_spln" target="_blank"><u>Master of Computing</u></a> (Computer Science Specialisation) and working on either a dissertation, or a 2-semester project related to cybersecurity.</li>
                    <li><a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/#mcomp_spln" target="_blank"><u>Master of Computing</u></a> (Infocomm Security Specialisation).</li>
 <li><a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/#mcomp_spln" target="_blank"><u>Master of Computing</u></a> (Artificial Intelligence Specialisation) and working on either a dissertation, or a 2-semester project related to cybersecurity.</li>
                    <li>Master programmes (by Research) from Faculty of Science, Faculty of Engineering and School of Computing with dissertation focused on cybersecurity.</li>
                </ul>
                <p>&nbsp;</p>

                <p><strong>APPLICATION PROCESS (For Master by Coursework)</strong></p>
                <ul><li><p>Application is by invitation. Eligible students will receive email invitation after they accepted offer from the respective Master of Computing programme.</p></li></ul>
               
 <p>&nbsp;</p>
 <p><strong>APPLICATION PROCESS (For Master by Research)</strong></p>
                <ul><li> <p>Applicants who have accepted offer from the respective Master programme indicate their interest by contacting us via email.</p></li></ul>

                <p>&nbsp;</p>
