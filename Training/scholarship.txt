                    <h2 class="train-sch-text mb-2">OVERVIEW</h2>
                    <p>The CSA-National Cybersecurity R&amp;D Lab Scholarship (NCLS) aims
                        to drive and support the development of professionals in Singapore’s
                        cybersecurity sector and support the National Cybersecurity R&amp;D Lab (NCL) initiative.
                        This scholarship (funded by the <a href="https://www.csa.gov.sg/">
                            Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA)</a>)
                        will be awarded to qualified students who are enrolled in NUS' Master’s
                        programmes focusing on cybersecurity.</p>
                    <p>&nbsp;</p>
                    <h2 class="train-sch-text mb-2">ELIGIBILITY</h2>
                    <p>This scholarship is open to students who:</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li class="train-sch-li">Are Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents.</li>
                        <li class="train-sch-li">Are admitted into NUS Masters programme with emphasis on cybersecurity (full-time or part-time).</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>&nbsp;</p>
                    <h2 class="train-sch-text mb-2">BENEFITS OF NCLS PROGRAMME</h2>
                    <ul>
                        <li class="train-sch-li">Tuition fees (after MOE Tuition Fee Grant) of NUS Master of Computing or related course programme.</li>
                        <li class="train-sch-li">Full-time students are eligible for monthly stipends capped at 18 months. The monthly stipend amount is $3500 for Singapore Citizens and $2700 for Singapore Permanent Residents.</li>
                        <li class="train-sch-li">A project grant of $5,000 to the academic supervisors of the students’ cybersecurity projects or dissertations.</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>&nbsp;</p>

                    <h2 class="train-sch-text mb-2">CONDITIONS OF NCLS</h2>
                    <p>Students who are awarded the scholarship must:</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li class="train-sch-li">Maintain their Cumulative Average Point (CAP) at average 4.0 and have good academic standing.</li>
                        <li class="train-sch-li">Post-graduation, be employed at NCL for a period of at least 1 year,
                            or embark on their PhD studies in a Singapore University,
                            or be employed in Singapore’s cybersecurity industry for at least 1 year.
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>&nbsp;</p>
                    <h2 class="train-sch-text mb-2">ELIGIBLE NUS MASTER’S PROGRAMMES</h2>
                    <ul>
                        <li class="train-sch-li">
                            <a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/#mcomp_spln" target="_blank">
                                <u>Master of Computing</u>
                                </a> (General Track) and working on either a dissertation, or a
                                2-semester project related to cybersecurity.
                        </li>
                        <li class="train-sch-li">
                            <a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/#mcomp_spln" target="_blank">
                                <u>Master of Computing</u>
                            </a>
                            (Computer Science Specialisation) and working on either a dissertation,
                            or a 2-semester project related to cybersecurity.
                        </li>
                        <li class="train-sch-li"><a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/#mcomp_spln" target="_blank">
                            <u>Master of Computing</u>
                        </a> (Infocomm Security Specialisation).
                        </li>
                        <li class="train-sch-li"><a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/#mcomp_spln" target="_blank">
                            <u>Master of Computing</u>
                        </a>
                            (Artificial Intelligence Specialisation) and working on either a dissertation,
                            or a 2-semester project related to cybersecurity.
                        </li>
                        <li class="train-sch-li"><a href="https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/pg/mis/" target="_blank">
                            <u>Master of Computing</u>
                        </a> (Information Systems Specialisation). </li>
                        <li>Master programmes (by Research) from Faculty of Science,
                            Faculty of Engineering and School of Computing with dissertation focused on cybersecurity.
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>&nbsp;</p>
